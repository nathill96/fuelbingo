Indy Fuel NHL Sweater Bingo

In protest of the Article 13 development in the European Union, Indy Fuel NHL Sweater Bingo will not be available for this weekend’s games. I strongly urge you to read on.

Article 13 is a proposed EU law intended to prevent copyright infringement on the internet. It requires online platforms such as YouTube, BandCamp, Soundcloud, itch.io, Medium, and any other sites that accept user-generated content, to prevent copyrighted material from being uploaded to their servers. In other words, if a user of any website posts infringing material, not only is the user punished, but the entire website.

Obviously we don’t live in Europe. However, YouTube, Bandcamp, and other internet platforms operate worldwide, so if the law passes, they will either be forced to cease operations in the EU, or comply with Article 13 regulations worldwide. In addition, if the law passes, a similar law will surely be proposed in the US.

YouTube has 300 hours of video uploaded to its servers every minute, so they could not possibly monitor that much content. Therefore, the only way they could comply with the law would be if YouTube stopped allowing everyone to submit their content, and restricted the platform to large, established companies.

Indianapolis is certainly not a city traditionally known for arts or music, but our arts scenes have grown tremendously in recent years in large part because of the open internet. Online platforms such as YouTube, Vimeo, BandCamp, and Soundcloud have empowered smaller artists and given them a platform to share their work with the world. If such platforms no longer allowed user-submitted works, smaller artists like those in our city will have almost no platform at all.

The law will be voted on next week. Of course there’s not much we can do, since we don’t live in Europe. If you happen to own a website and be reading this before March 21st, please black-out your site on that day, and explain why. In addition, anyone can sign this petition through Change.org. Thank you and go Fuel.
